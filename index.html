Our Story

STRAD Consultants,
is a professional structural design and civil engineering consultancy committed to delivering innovative, economical, and safe design solutions.

We combine technical excellence with practical experience to ensure that every project we undertake stands the test of time — both structurally and economically.

Core Philosophy --
We believe in building structures that balance strength, serviceability, and sustainability while ensuring design efficiency and costeffectiveness for our clients.

Vision

To be recognized as a trusted structural design partner known for excellence, innovation, and reliability across the built environment sector.

Mission

  • Deliver high-quality structural designs conforming to national and international codes.
  • Optimize material and design for cost efficiency.
  • Maintain transparency, commitment, and timely project delivery.
  • Continuously evolve through technology and research-driven practices.

Codes & Standards Followed

We adhere to all relevant national and international codes and standards, including both established guidelines and newly updated regulations. Our designs strictly comply with Indian Standards (IS codes) for concrete, steel, and seismic design, as well as international best practices. We ensure that every project meets the highest regulatory requirements and industry standards, staying current with the latest amendments and revisions to maintain the highest level of structural integrity and safety.
